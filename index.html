The life of Sushant

Sushant Singh Rajput was born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar, India. He was an actor, dancer, and model.He graduated from the National Institute of Technology, Delhi, with a degree in engineering.

Rajput began his acting career in 2008 with the television series Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil. He then starred in several other television series, including Pavitra Rishta and Zee Horror Show. In 2013, he made his Bollywood debut with the film Kai Po Che!. He went on to star in several other successful films, including Shuddh Desi Romance, PK, and MS Dhoni: The Untold Story.

Rajput was a talented actor and dancer. He was also known for his fitness and his love of animals. He was a role model for many young people in India.

On June 14, 2020, Rajput was found dead in his apartment in Mumbai. His death was ruled a suicide. His death was a shock to the Indian film industry and to his fans around the world.
